# Barclays - React Native Engineer

Join us as an React Native Engineer at Barclays, where you will spearhead the evolution of our infrastructure and deployment pipelines, driving innovation and operational excellence. You will harness cutting-edge technology to build and manage robust, scalable and secure infrastructure, ensuring seamless delivery of our digital solutions.  

**Location:** Pune

## Purpose of the role

To design, develop and improve software, utilising various engineering methodologies, that provides business, platform, and technology capabilities for our customers and colleagues. 

## To be successful as a React Native Engineer, you should have experience with:

* Develop and deliver high‑quality, maintainable and performant software using React Native and associated technologies.
* Contribute to the design and implementation of mobile customer journeys aligned to Barclays UK digital architecture.
* Collaborate with product managers, designers, architects and engineers to define requirements and deliver integrated solutions.
* Participate actively in code reviews, ensuring adherence to engineering standards and best practices.
* Apply secure coding practices to protect customer data and meet regulatory and risk requirements.
* Implement and maintain unit and component testing to ensure reliability, readability and quality of code.
* Support production stability through effective debugging, monitoring and issue resolution.
* Stay informed of industry and technology trends, contributing to engineering communities and continuous improvement initiatives.
* Deliver work in line with Barclays Values and Mindset, taking ownership of risk and control responsibilities.
* Strong experience with React Native, JavaScript and/or TypeScript.
* Experience building cross‑platform mobile applications for iOS and Android.
* Good understanding of React concepts, component‑based design and state management.
* Experience integrating mobile applications with RESTful APIs.
* Hands‑on experience with unit and component testing frameworks.
* Familiarity with source control (Git) and CI/CD pipelines.
* Experience working in Agile/Scrum delivery environments.
* Perform assigned activities independently and to a consistently high standard.
* Demonstrate in‑depth technical knowledge within the React Native and mobile engineering domain.
* Apply sound judgement based on analysis of information and established engineering patterns.
* Influence of decision-making within own area of expertise.
* Take ownership for managing risk and strengthening controls in relation to work delivered.
* Act as a technical advisor within the team where appropriate.
* Have an impact on related teams and collaborate effectively across functions.
